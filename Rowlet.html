Rowlet

The Grass Quill Pokémon

Rowlet is a grass/flying type Pokémon from Generation VII.

Rowlet evolves into Dratrix at lvl 17, Who evolves into Decidueye at level 34.
Along with Litten and Popplio, Rowlet is one of three starter Pokémon of Alola available at the beginning of Pokémon Sun, Moon, Ultra Sun, and Ultra Moon.

Rowlet is a nocturnal Pokémon, so it stores energy during the day through photosynthesis. Rowlet approaches opponents by silently gliding, before unleashing a volley of kicks. This Pokémon is able to rotate its head almost 180 degrees and has excellent night vision. Its feathers are as sharp as blades, which it launches at foes from the air. Rowlet feel most relaxed in tight, dark spaces.
