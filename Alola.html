Alola

The Island Region


The Alola region is a region in the pokemon universe. It is the seventh region in the series and is the setting of Pokemon sun and moon.

Alola is made up of five different islands, There are four natural islands and one artificial. The four natrural islands are Melemele Island, Akala Island, Ula'ula Island en Poni Island. These islands are named after colors in hawaiian. Melemele means Yellow, Akala means pink, Ula'ula means red and poni means purple. The artificial island is Aether Paradise. Alola is a populair vaktion destination and so attrects a lot of tourists from the other regions.

Starter Pokemon

The starter pokemon from Alola are Rowlet, Litten and Popplio.
